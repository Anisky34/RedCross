Benefits of Donating Blood

Save Lives

A single donation can save up to 3 lives and help people who are currently in trauma, surgery, cancer treatments, blood disorders, and much more

Free Health Screening

Each donation includes a mini-physical and vital signs check

Calorie Burn

Donating blood burns about 600-700 calories to replace the blood you lost

Reduce Heart Disease Risk

Regular blood donation is associated with lower blood viscosity, which basically means that it can help with reducing the risk of hearth attacks

Psychological Benefits

You feel better helping other people type shi

Community Connection

Blood donation connects you to the people around you in your community

Requirements to be able to Donate Blood

Basic Requirements

  • Age:Must be at least 17 yrs old(16 w/ permission from parents)
  • Weight:At least 110 lbs
  • Health:Should be feeling good and healthy
  • Time:At least a 56 day interval since your last blood donation

Potential Restrictions

Some conditions or situations may make you not be able to donate blood

  • Recent illness, fever, or infection
  • Certain medications
  • Low iron levels
  • Traveled to other countries recently

What to expect when Donating

  1. Registration: Sign in and Show ID
  2. Health History:Answer questions about your health history in a private setting
  3. Mini-Physical:Temperature, pulse, blood pressure, and hemoglobin check
  4. Donation:The actual donation is bout 8-10 min
  5. RefreshmentsEnjoy snacks and drinks while resting

The whole process takes about an hour, with the actual donating being 10 min long

Possible side effects

Most people experience no side effects but if you do it would probably be

  • Lightheadedness or dizziness
  • Bruising at the needle site
  • Fatigue
  • Arm pain or soreness

Our staff is going to minimize this so don't worry too much

Prepare for your donation

  • Eat a health meal and drink plenty of water
  • Wear a shirt that can be rolled up
  • Bring ID and a list of medications you're taking
  • Get a good nights sleep
