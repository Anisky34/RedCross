Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteering with Hillcrest Red Cross is a rewarding way to give back to our school and the community

Join Our Club!

Join our club where you can learn anything ranging from how to deal with emergency situations to how to recognize them

  • Attend Hillcrest High School
  • Have your Tuesdays be free from 2:30-3:30

Blood Drive Volunteer

Help run local blood drives by greeting donors, assisting with registration, and providing refreshments

Requirements

  • 16 yrs or older
  • Be friendly
  • If you wanna work registration have some basic computer skills

Time Commitment 4-6 hour shifts, will happen at school twice this year, elsewhere look at redcross website

Help with local Redcross building

Assist with the local redcross chapter about .2 miles away from us, you can help them with stuff such as data entry, filing, answering phones, and volunteer schedules

Requirements

  • 16 or older
  • Basic computer skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Professional communication skills

Time Commitment:4-hour shifts, weekdays after school besides tuesdays come in whenever you want
